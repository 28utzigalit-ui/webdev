Cats are independent, playful, and curious animals that are loved by people all around the world. They are known for their soft fur, sharp senses, and unique personalities. Some cats enjoy lounging in the sun all day, while others love to explore, climb, and chase toys. Whether they are calm or energetic, cats make great companions and often form strong bonds with their owners.
